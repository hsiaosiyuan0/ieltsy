# 第 12 章：常见易错点 Common Errors

> 共 17 点｜★★★ 7 / ★★ 9 / ★ 1
>
> Band 7 高频失分点。中文母语者最容易踩的坑。

## §20 易错点详细列表

214. `who` vs `whom` ★ — `whom` 用于宾格（口语中常被 `who` 替代）
215. `that` vs `which` ★★ — 限定（that）vs 非限定（which，前加逗号）
216. `fewer` vs `less` ★★ — 可数（fewer books）vs 不可数（less time）
217. `between` vs `among` ★★ — 两者 vs 三者及以上
218. `its` vs `it's` ★★★ — 物主代词 vs `it is` 缩写
219. `affect` vs `effect` / `then` vs `than` ★★★ — affect 动词"影响" / effect 名词"效果"；then 时间 / than 比较
220. `lay` (放) vs `lie` (躺) vs `lie` (说谎) ★★ — lay/laid/laid (vt) / lie/lay/lain (vi 躺) / lie/lied/lied (vi 说谎)
221. `raise` (及物) vs `rise` (不及物) ★★ — raise prices / prices rise
222. `since` vs `for` ★★★ — since + 时间起点 / for + 时长
223. `been` vs `gone` ★★ — has been to (去过)vs has gone to (去了，未回)
224. 双重否定的误用 ★★ — `I don't know nothing` ✗（标准英语视为不地道）
225. 悬垂修饰（dangling modifier）★★ — `Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.` ✗（修饰词主语与主句主语不一致）
226. `one of the + 复数 + who/that` 后动词用复数 ★★ — `He is one of the students who **are** here.`
227. 比较对象一致 ★★★ — `My salary is higher than my colleague's ✓`（不能直接 than my colleague）/ `than that of his colleague ✓`
228. 冠词漏掉 ★★★（**中文母语者最大坑** — 抽象名词、单数可数名词前必须有限定词）
229. 单复数混用 ★★★（especially after `a / an / one / each / every` 必须接单数；`many / several / few` 接复数）
230. 时态混乱 ★★★（writing 中无故切换时态 — Band 7 写作硬伤）

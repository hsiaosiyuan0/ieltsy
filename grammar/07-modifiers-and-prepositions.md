## §14 介词 Prepositions

158. 时间介词 ★★★ — 时间点用 `at`、日期用 `on`、较长时段用 `in`；`by` 表截止完成，`until` 表持续到某时，`since / for / during / while` 分别连接起点、时长、名词时段与分句；例：`Please send the form by Friday, but stay here until six.`
159. 地点介词 ★★★ — `at` 把地点看作一个点或活动场所，`in` 表边界内部，`on` 表表面或线路；再按空间关系选择 `above / over / below / under / among / between`；例：`I'll meet you at the station, inside the café on the first floor.`
160. 方式 / 工具介词 ★★ — `by` / `with` / `through` / `via`
161. 介词搭配（动词 + 介词）★★★ — `depend on / consist of / result in / contribute to / cope with / account for / refer to / lead to / object to`；搭配中的 `to` 是介词时后接名词或 `V-ing`，不是不定式标记；例：`I look forward to seeing you.`
162. 介词搭配（形容词 + 介词）★★★ — aware of / capable of / responsible for / concerned about / different from / similar to
163. 介词搭配（名词 + 介词）★★★ — increase in / decrease in / cause of / reason for / effect on / impact on / approach to / solution to
164. 介词短语作状语 / 定语 / 表语 ★★

## §47 程度、补语与复合修饰 Degree · Complements · Compounds

418. 可分级 / 不可分级形容词与强化词搭配 ★★★ — `very / really cold`，但 `absolutely / completely freezing`；避免机械搭配 `very freezing`；例：`The room was very cold, but the water was absolutely freezing.`
419. `too / enough + to`-infinitive / `for` ★★★ — `too + adj + to V / adj + enough + to V / enough + noun + for sb` 表超限或足量；例：`The rent is too high for students to afford.`
420. `as` vs `like` ★★★ — `as + clause / 身份`，`like + noun phrase`；非正式口语中 `like` 也可接分句；例：`As a teacher, she explains ideas like an expert.`
452. 形容词补语：介词、不定式与 `that`-从句 ★★★ — `aware of / eager to / certain that` 等补语由形容词决定；例：`Residents are aware of the risk and eager to hear what the council decides.`
453. 立场、确定性与聚焦副词的位置 ★★★ — `apparently / arguably / probably / even / only / especially` 的位置会改变作用范围和语气；例：`The policy will probably help, but it will only affect new applicants.`
454. 复合形容词与多词前置修饰 ★★ — 数字、分词和副词可构成 `a five-year plan / a well-designed system / rapidly growing demand`；例：`A well-designed public transport system can support a fast-growing city.`

## §48 路径与复杂介词 Path & Complex Prepositions

421. 移动与方向介词 ★★★ — `to` 表目标、`into / onto` 表越过边界后的终点，`across` 表横跨表面，`through` 表穿过内部；结合 `out of / along / past / towards` 精确表达路线；例：`Walk through the park, across the bridge, and into the station.`
422. 复合介词与学术关系框架 ★★ — `in terms of / with regard to / in accordance with / on behalf of` 限定评价维度或关系；例：`In terms of cost, the second option is preferable.`

## 语法笔记 Grammar Notes

### 158. 时间介词

#### 核心判断

先判断你表达的是**时间点、日期、时段、截止点、持续终点、起点还是时长**，不要只靠中文的“在、到、从”逐字翻译。

- `at 7:30 / at noon / at night`：钟点或具体时刻。
- `on Monday / on July 21 / on Monday morning`：日期和具体某一天。
- `in July / in 2026 / in the morning`：月份、年份和较长时段。
- `by Friday`：最迟周五完成，动作可更早发生。
- `until Friday`：状态或动作一直持续到周五。
- `since 2024 / for two years`：完成体中的起点与持续长度。
- `during the meeting / while we were meeting`：前者接名词短语，后者接分句。

#### 生活语境

- 家庭安排：`We need to leave by eight, but the children can sleep until seven.`
- 工作沟通：`I have worked from home since Monday and will stay here for the rest of the week.`
- 看病预约：`I fell asleep during the scan while the nurse was checking the results.`
- 出行：`The last train leaves at 10:40 on Friday night.`

#### 最小对立与纠错

- `Please return the key by six.`：六点前归还。
- `Please keep the key until six.`：一直保留到六点。
- `I have lived here since three years.` ✗ → `I have lived here for three years.` ✓
- `during I was cooking` ✗ → `while I was cooking` ✓ / `during dinner` ✓

#### 语域迁移

这些区别在生活对话、邮件截止时间和 IELTS Task 1 时间范围中都一样。写作中尤其要避免用 `until` 代替表示完成期限的 `by`。

### 159. 地点介词

#### 核心判断

`at / in / on` 体现说话人怎样观察地点：把它看成一个活动点、一个有边界的内部，还是一个表面或线路。

- `at the station / at work / at home`：关注地点或活动，而非内部结构。
- `in the station / in the office / in Shanghai`：位于边界或区域内部。
- `on the table / on the wall / on the bus route`：接触表面或位于线路、楼层。
- `above` 只表示更高；`over` 还可表示覆盖或跨越。
- `below` 只表示更低；`under` 常表示正下方、覆盖或控制关系。
- `between` 强调可区分的对象；`among` 强调群体内部。

#### 生活语境

- 见面：`I'll wait at the supermarket, near the entrance.`
- 居家：`The spare key is in the drawer under the kettle.`
- 办公：`Our meeting room is on the third floor, between the lift and the kitchen.`
- 导航：`There is a pharmacy across the road from the clinic.`

#### 最小对立与纠错

- `I'm at the hospital.`：把医院当作目的地或活动场所。
- `I'm in the hospital.`：明确人在建筑内部；美式英语还常暗示住院，需结合语境。
- `The lamp is above the table.` 不一定正对桌面；`The lamp is over the table.` 通常暗示在其正上方。
- `in the bus` △ → 乘坐公共汽车通常说 `on the bus` ✓。

#### 语域迁移

口语中先保证视角清楚；地图题和流程描述中则要稳定区分位置与移动，避免一张图里随意交换 `in / into` 或 `on / onto`。

### 161. 介词搭配（动词 + 介词）

#### 核心判断

先把动词和介词当作一个搭配单位记忆，再判断介词后的补语。`to` 有两种身份：不定式标记后接动词原形，介词后接名词、代词或 `V-ing`。

- 不定式 `to`：`want to leave / plan to study / decide to wait`。
- 介词 `to`：`object to the plan / contribute to improving it / look forward to seeing you`。
- 判断方法：如果 `to` 前的动词或短语本身要求一个对象或方向关系，它通常是介词；可先用名词替换测试，如 `look forward to the weekend`。

#### 生活语境

- 工作：`We need to respond to the email before lunch.`
- 学习：`I am committed to practising for twenty minutes a day.`
- 家庭：`Everyone contributed to cleaning the kitchen.`
- 社交：`I'm looking forward to meeting your parents.`

#### 最小对立与纠错

- `I used to cook.`：过去常常做饭。
- `I am used to cooking.`：已经习惯做饭。
- `I look forward to see you.` ✗ → `I look forward to seeing you.` ✓
- `The change resulted from the delay.` 是原因来源；`The delay resulted in a change.` 是导致结果。

#### 语域迁移

介词搭配不靠“更正式”决定，而靠中心动词的选择。口语和写作都应优先使用自己能稳定控制的搭配，不要为了复杂度临时替换介词。

### 421. 移动与方向介词

#### 核心判断

移动介词描述路径的不同部分：目标、进入、离开、横跨表面、穿过内部、沿线移动或经过某点。

- `to the shop`：目标是商店，不说明是否进入。
- `into the shop / onto the platform`：强调越过边界，到达内部或表面。
- `across the road / through the tunnel`：横跨开放表面与穿过有内部的空间。
- `along the river / past the bank / towards home`：沿线、经过和朝向。

#### 生活语境

- 通勤：`I walked through the station and onto the platform.`
- 买菜：`Go across the car park and into the supermarket.`
- 居家：`The child ran out of the bedroom and towards the kitchen.`
- 周末：`We cycled along the river and past the old library.`

#### 最小对立与纠错

- `She went to the kitchen.`：把厨房作为目标。
- `She went into the kitchen.`：明确进入厨房。
- `We drove across the tunnel.` ✗ → `We drove through the tunnel.` ✓
- `He jumped on the bed.` 可表示在床上跳；`He jumped onto the bed.` 强调从别处跳到床上。

#### 语域迁移

日常导航需要让听者能复原路线；IELTS 地图与流程任务还要求从一致视角描述路径。先选对空间关系，再考虑更复杂的句式。

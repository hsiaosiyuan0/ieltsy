## §15 主谓一致 Subject-Verb Agreement

165. 集合名词作主语 ★★（family / team — 视作整体或个体）
166. 不定代词作主语 ★★★ — `each / every / either / neither / none / no one / everyone / everybody` 视为单数
167. 就近原则 ★★★ — `either...or` / `neither...nor` / `not only...but also` / `there be`
168. 数量短语作主语 ★★★ — `a number of`（复数）vs `the number of`（单数）；`a great deal of` / `plenty of` / `the majority of`
169. 不可数名词作主语 ★★★
170. 主语后置时的一致 ★★ — `Here is...` / `There is...`
171. 算术 / 距离 / 时间 / 金额 ★★ — 视为整体（单数）
172. `and` 连接的主语 ★★（视情况单复数 — A and B 整体 vs 两件事）

## §16 间接引语 Reported Speech

173. 时态后移规则 + 例外 ★★（普遍真理 / 主句现在时）
174. 人称 / 时间 / 地点状语的转换 ★★
175. 一般疑问句 → `if / whether` ★★
176. 特殊疑问句 → `wh-` 引导的宾语从句 ★★
177. 祈使句 → `tell / ask / order / advise sb (not) to do` ★★
178. 转述动词的选择 ★★（claim / argue / suggest / propose / recommend / urge / acknowledge / deny / point out）

## §17 句子类型与结构 Sentence Types

179. 简单句 / 并列句 / 复合句 / 并列复合句 ★★
180. 陈述 / 疑问 / 祈使 / 感叹 ★★★
181. 疑问句类型 ★★★ — yes/no / wh- / 选择 / 反意
182. 反意疑问句尾巴的规则与例外 ★★（`I am... aren't I?` / `Let's..., shall we?`）
183. 句子长度与结构多样性 ★★★（Band 7 要求）

## §26 疑问句深入 Questions In Depth

### 嵌入式 / 间接疑问句
291. 不倒装规则 ★★★ — `Could you tell me where the station is?`（不是 where is the station）
292. yes/no 用 `if / whether` ★★ — `Do you know if she's coming?`
293. 嵌入到陈述句 ★★ — `I wonder why he left.` / `I'm not sure what to do.`
294. 嵌入到从句 ★★ — `She asked me what I wanted.`

### 否定疑问句
295. 表惊讶 / 抱怨 / 求确认 ★★ — `Don't you like it?` / `Aren't you tired?`
296. 修辞性否定疑问 ★ — `Isn't it obvious?`

### 反意疑问句的难点
297. 助动词类型匹配 + 否定肯定颠倒 ★★
298. 反意疑问例外 ★★ — `I am... aren't I?` / `Let's..., shall we?` / `Let me/him/them..., will you?` / 祈使句 → `will you / won't you / can you` / `There is..., isn't there?` / `Nothing/Nobody/Few/Little/Hardly/Seldom 视为否定 → 用肯定尾巴`

### 修辞疑问句
299. 修辞疑问 ★ — `Who knows?` / `Why bother?` / `What's the point?`

## §28 平行结构 Parallelism

> Band 7 写作硬指标。三个或以上的并列单位形式必须一致。

305. 平行的语法层面 ★★★ — 名词 / 动词 / 动名词 / 不定式 / 介词短语 / 从句 / 形容词
306. 并列名词 / 形容词 ★★★ — `reading, writing, and listening` / `intelligent, hard-working, and creative`
307. 并列动词 ★★★ — `He came, saw, and conquered.`
308. 并列不定式 / 动名词 ★★★ — `to study, to travel, and to learn`（或省去后两个 to）/ `swimming, jogging, and running`
309. 并列从句 ★★ — `He said that he was tired and that he needed rest.`
310. 关联连词的平行 ★★★ — `not only...but also` / `either...or` / `both...and` 后接的成分必须形式一致
311. 比较结构的平行 ★★★ — `My salary is higher than my colleague's ✓` / `than my colleague ✗`；`than that of his colleague ✓`

## §29 句子边界错误 Sentence Boundary

> Band 7 评分语 "frequent error-free sentences" 卡这一类错误最多。

312. 句子片段（Sentence Fragment）★★ — `Because he was tired. ✗`（从句不能单独成句）/ `The students studying for the exam. ✗`（缺谓语）
313. 连接错误（Run-on / Fused Sentence）★★ — `I love IELTS it's challenging. ✗`
314. 逗号粘连（Comma Splice）★★★ — `I love IELTS, it's challenging. ✗`（独立分句仅用逗号连接）
315. 五种修复方式 ★★ —
   - 加句号: `I love IELTS. It's challenging.`
   - 加分号: `I love IELTS; it's challenging.`
   - 加并列连词: `I love IELTS, and it's challenging.`
   - 改主从复合句: `I love IELTS because it's challenging.`
   - 副词性连词 + 分号: `I love IELTS; however, it's challenging.`

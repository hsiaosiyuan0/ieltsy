134. 不规则复数 ★★ — phenomena / criteria / data / media / analyses / hypotheses
135. 物质名词的可数化 ★ — `a coffee` / `two beers`

137. `a / an` 元音音素 ★★★（an hour / a university）
138. `the` 的特指、类指与专名规则 ★★★ — 用 `the + noun` 指听者可识别、语境中唯一或已限定的对象，也用于部分地理名称和类别整体；例：`Could you close the window next to the sofa?`

462. 不定冠词的意义选择：`a / an + singular countable noun` ★★★ — 用于首次引入、职业或类别成员、单位频率和“某一个”；例：`My sister is a nurse, and she works four days a week.`
463. 首次提及、后续特指与指代链：`a/an → the → it/this` ★★★ — 先用不定冠词引入新对象，再用定冠词或代词维持共同指称；例：`I found a café near the station. The café is quiet, so I often study there.`

## §45 名词形态与所有格 Noun Morphology & Possession

410. 规则复数、拼写变化与同形复数 ★★★ — `cats / buses / cities` 按拼写规则变化，`sheep / species` 单复数同形；例：`Several species are threatened.`
411. 专有名称与群体名称的数 ★★ — 区分 `the Netherlands is / the Alps are / the Smiths`，同时控制专名大小写与主谓一致；例：`The United States is a large country, while the Alps are a mountain range.`

## §46 复杂名词短语与指代 Complex Noun Phrases & Reference

414. 名词前置修饰与复合名词 ★★★ — `data analysis / car park / a two-year plan`；前置名词通常用单数，数字复合修饰语用连字符；例：`The city launched a three-year transport plan.`
415. 扩展名词短语：前置与后置修饰 ★★★ — `determiner + premodifier + head + PP / relative / participle` 提高信息密度；例：`The rapid rise in housing costs recorded last year has slowed.`
416. `another / other / others / the other / the others` ★★★ — 区分追加、泛指剩余与特指剩余，并判断后面是否还需名词；例：`One option is cheap; the other is faster, while others are safer.`
417. 限定集合的 `of`-量词：`most / none / each / both of` ★★★ — 无 `of` 常泛指，`quantifier + of + determiner/pronoun` 从已知集合中取部分，并按中心量词或实际所指控制一致；例：`Most parents agreed, but none of them was ready to leave.`
449. 泛指人称：`one / you / they / one's` ★★ — 用于一般经验或不特指的施事，正式度与包容性不同；例：`One should check one's sources, although in conversation you would usually say they.`
450. 比较中的替代：`that / those of` ★★ — 用单数 `that` 或复数 `those` 避免重复名词，并保持比较对象一致；例：`The cost of rail travel is lower than that of air travel.`
458. 非肯定环境中的 `any / anyone / ever / yet` ★★★ — 常用于否定、疑问、条件和比较，肯定句中的 `any` 可表示“任一”；例：`If anyone has any questions, they can ask at any time.`

## 语法笔记 Grammar Notes

### 138. `the` 的特指、类指与专名规则

#### 核心判断

使用 `the` 前先问：**听者能不能根据共同知识、前文或名词后的限定信息，确定我说的是哪一个 / 哪一些？** 能确定时通常用 `the`。

- 前文已出现：`I saw a film last night. The film was surprisingly moving.`
- 当下情境可识别：`Please turn off the light.`（双方知道是哪盏灯）
- 世界或局部语境中唯一：`the sun / the kitchen in our flat`
- 后置成分明确限定：`the report that you sent me / the café near our office`
- 序数词与最高级通常具有唯一性：`the first train / the cheapest option`

`the + singular noun` 也可正式地把一种动物、发明或乐器作为类别整体讨论：`The smartphone has changed how families communicate.` 日常泛指更常用复数：`Smartphones are useful.`

#### 专名规则

多数人名、城市、街道、单个国家、单座山峰和单个湖泊不用冠词：`Mia / Shanghai / Oxford Street / China / Mount Fuji / Lake Victoria`。以下名称通常带 `the`：

- 河流、海洋、海峡和山脉：`the Yangtze / the Pacific / the English Channel / the Alps`
- 复数或含政治实体词的国名：`the Netherlands / the United States / the United Kingdom`
- 部分机构、酒店、剧院和报刊：`the United Nations / the Ritz / the Guardian`

#### 对比与纠错

- `I need a charger.`：任意一个充电器都可以。
- `I need the charger on your desk.`：被后置短语限定，双方能确定。
- `The life is expensive in this city. ✗` → `Life is expensive in this city. ✓`：抽象概念泛指不用 `the`。

口语中，情境让对象显而易见时可直接说 `the door / the kids / the car`。正式写作不能假定读者共享私人语境，应先引入对象或用后置修饰说明指哪一个。

### 139. 零冠词的泛指、机构与专名规则

#### 核心判断

零冠词写作 `Ø`，也就是名词前不放冠词。它最常表达“不是某一个，而是这一类事物或概念本身”。

- `Ø + plural countable noun`：`Children need regular sleep.`
- `Ø + uncountable noun`：`Public transport saves energy.`
- 多数专名、语言、学科、月份、三餐和运动：`Emma studies English on Monday before lunch and plays tennis afterwards.`
- `by + Ø transport`：`We usually go to work by bus.`

#### 机构功能与具体地点

`school / university / bed / work / prison / church` 等词表示其通常制度功能或活动时常用零冠词；表示具体建筑、地点或物体时用限定词。

- `My daughter starts school in September.`：开始接受学校教育。
- `I went to the school to meet her teacher.`：去了那所学校的建筑。
- `The children are in bed.`：在睡觉或准备睡觉。
- `There is a bag on the bed.`：指房间里的具体床。

`hospital` 存在地域差异：英式英语常说 `in hospital` 表示住院，美式英语通常说 `in the hospital`。IELTS 中两种规范变体都可以，但同一篇文本应保持一致。

#### 最小对立与语域

- `Coffee is expensive here.`：咖啡这种商品泛指。
- `The coffee in this café is expensive.`：特指这家店的咖啡。
- `I have breakfast at seven.`：日常一餐。
- `The breakfast at the hotel was excellent.`：被地点限定的具体早餐。

`The young need support` 这类 `the + adjective` 可指一个群体，语气较概括；日常表达常用更直接、包容的 `young people`。

### 417. 限定集合的 `of`-量词：`most / none / each / both of`

#### 核心判断与形式

直接放在名词前的量词常谈一个泛指类别；`of` 结构则从**已经限定或双方已知的集合**中取出全部或部分。

- 泛指：`Most parents worry about screen time.`
- 限定集合：`Most of the parents in our class joined the meeting.`
- 结构：`quantifier + of + the/these/my + noun` 或 `quantifier + of + object pronoun`
- `most of people ✗` → `most people ✓` / `most of the people ✓`

常见搭配包括 `all/both/each/either/neither/most/some/none of`。`all` 和 `both` 在部分名词短语中可省略 `of`：`all (of) my notes / both (of) the shops`；代词前不能省略：`all of us / both of them`。

#### 谓语一致

- `both of + plural` 接复数：`Both of the buses are late.`
- `each/either/neither of + plural` 在规范正式英语中通常接单数：`Each of the rooms has a desk.`
- `most/some/all/none of` 按 `of` 后所指决定：`Most of the cake is gone.` / `Most of the guests are here.`
- `none of` 口语中可接单数或复数；正式写作可按“一个也没有”用单数，或按复数所指用复数，但同一上下文要自然一致。

家庭场景可说 `Both of the children are asleep.`，工作场景可说 `Most of our team works remotely.`，购物时可说 `None of these shirts fits me.` 正式数据写作要明确总体，例如 `Most of the respondents in the 18–25 group preferred online services.`

### 462. 不定冠词的意义选择：`a / an + singular countable noun`

#### 核心判断

`a/an` 只能直接限定**单数可数名词**。它不是单纯等于中文的“一个”，而是把某人或某物作为“某一个尚未特指的类别成员”引入谈话。`a` 还是 `an` 由后面单词开头的**音素**决定，具体发音规则见 137。

#### 形式与意义

- 首次引入：`We found a small playground near our home.`
- 身份、职业或类别成员：`My brother is an engineer.`
- 单位、频率或比率：`twice a week / £4 a kilo / 60 kilometres an hour`
- 表示“某一个，但不说明是哪一个”：`A colleague left this note for you.`
- 感叹和数量短语：`What a relief! / a hundred people / a few minutes`

#### 生活语境

- 家庭：`We need a bigger table for family dinners.`
- 工作：`I have a meeting with a new client this afternoon.`
- 学习：`Could you recommend an English podcast for beginners?`
- 购物与出行：`I bought a bottle of water before catching a train.`

#### 最小对立与纠错

- `I spoke to a manager.`：某位经理，身份尚未明确。
- `I spoke to the manager.`：双方知道是哪位经理，或语境中唯一。
- `She is teacher. ✗` → `She is a teacher. ✓`
- `an useful app ✗` → `a useful app ✓`：`useful` 以辅音音素 /j/ 开头。
- `an information ✗` → `some information / a piece of information ✓`：不可数名词不能直接接 `a/an`。

#### 口语与写作语域

日常口语里 `a` 常弱读，重点落在名词；需要强调“恰好一个”时用 `one`：`I said one ticket, not two.` 正式写作中首次引入研究、措施或趋势也可用 `a/an`，但后文必须用清楚的限定或指代维持对象。

### 463. 首次提及、后续特指与指代链：`a/an → the → it/this`

#### 核心判断

英语会通过冠词和代词标记信息状态：**新对象先引入，已建立的对象再追踪**。典型指代链是：

1. `a/an + noun` 引入一个新对象；
2. `the + same noun` 表示“刚才那个”；
3. `it/they` 在指向无歧义时避免重复；
4. `this/that + noun` 可回指并加入说话者的评价或开启下一步说明。

#### 生活语境

- 家庭：`We bought a lamp for the study. The lamp is adjustable, so the children can use it at night.`
- 工作：`A customer reported a payment problem. The problem affected her order, and we fixed it immediately.`
- 学习：`I watched a lecture on pronunciation. The lecture included a quiz, which helped me remember it.`
- 出行：`There is a bus at 8:10. The bus goes directly to the airport, but it is often crowded.`

首次提及并不永远用 `a/an`：不可数名词用 `some/Ø`，复数新对象可用 `some`，本来唯一或已被限定的对象直接用 `the`：`I checked the weather before we left.`

#### 指代清晰与错误修正

- `I bought a kettle and a toaster. It was expensive.`：`it` 指谁不清楚。
- `I bought a kettle and a toaster. The toaster was expensive.`：重新说出中心名词，消除歧义。
- `I saw a dog. A dog followed me home. ✗`：第二句像是在引入另一只狗。
- `I saw a dog. The dog followed me home. ✓`

#### 口语与写作语域

口语依靠共同现场，常从 `this/that` 开始：`Pass me that bag.` 写作没有手势和现场，必须用冠词、后置限定和明确先行词建立链条。段落隔得较远或存在多个可能先行词时，宁可重复一个简短名词短语，也不要使用含混的 `it/they/this`。

### 464. 不可数名词的单位量词（measure partitives）

#### 核心判断

不可数名词不能直接与数词或 `a/an` 连用。需要计数时，把可数的**单位、容器、形状或项目**作为中心名词：

`number + countable unit + of + uncountable noun`

复数变化落在单位词上，后面的不可数名词保持原形：`two pieces of advice`，不是 `two piece of advices`。

#### 常用类型

- 一般项目：`a piece of advice/information/news/furniture/equipment`
- 少量：`a bit of time/help/luck`（偏口语）
- 容器：`a cup of tea / two bottles of water / three bags of rice`
- 形状或切分：`a slice of bread / a bar of chocolate / a sheet of paper`
- 成套或成双物品：`a pair of trousers/glasses/scissors`
- 正式清单项目：`an item of expenditure/evidence/equipment`

#### 生活语境

- 家庭：`We need three pieces of furniture for the children's room.`
- 工作：`Could you send me two pieces of information before the meeting?`
- 学习：`The teacher gave me a useful piece of advice about pronunciation.`
- 购物：`Please add a loaf of bread and two cartons of milk to the order.`
- 出行：`Each passenger may carry one piece of hand luggage.`

#### 最小对立与纠错

- `two coffees`：口语中把咖啡可数化，通常指两杯。
- `two cups of coffee`：明确计量单位，更适合菜单之外的精确说明。
- `many equipments ✗` → `a lot of equipment / several pieces of equipment ✓`
- `an advice ✗` → `a piece of advice ✓`

#### 口语与写作语域

量词选择会改变实际含义：`a glass of chicken` 不合常识，`a piece of chicken` 才自然。口语常用宽泛的 `piece/bit`，合同、数据或学术写作应选择可核验的单位，如 `item`, `litre`, `kilogram`，避免含糊。

# 2026-06-02 · ARGUMENTATIVE · Why Schools Will Need Rules for Corporate AI Tools

> 体裁: argumentative | 字数: ~230 | 新词: 17/17 | 语法点: 一般将来时 #3

## 短文

① In the next decade, corporate AI tools will dominate many classrooms unless schools set clear rules.
② Some people perceive this change as a threat, but under the right circumstances it will also create considerable potential benefits for learning.
③ The key issue is not whether technology will enter education; it is which component of teaching it will replace and which it will support.
④ Schools are going to seek tools that can select relevant exercises for each student and demonstrate weak points instantly.
⑤ These systems will contribute to better feedback, especially when teachers face constant pressure and limited time.
⑥ However, education should not be designed around corporate profit.
⑦ Governments will need to restrict the collection of student data and exclude companies that cannot ensure privacy.
⑧ Teachers shall remain the final decision-makers, because they understand context, emotion, and progress better than any platform.
⑨ A balanced policy will give emphasis to human judgement while still allowing useful software to assist routine practice.
⑩ If schools adopt this approach, AI will not replace teachers; it will become one practical tool among many.
⑪ Therefore, the question is not whether AI is coming, but how we are going to govern it before it governs us.

## 中文翻译

① 在未来十年里，企业 AI 工具会支配许多课堂，除非学校制定清晰规则。
② 有些人把这种变化看成威胁，但在合适的条件下，它也会为学习创造相当大的潜在益处。
③ 关键问题不是技术是否会进入教育，而是它会替代教学中的哪一部分，又会支持哪一部分。
④ 学校将会寻找能够为每个学生选择相关练习、并即时显示薄弱点的工具。
⑤ 这些系统将有助于提供更好的反馈，尤其是在教师面对持续压力和有限时间的时候。
⑥ 然而，教育不应围绕企业利润来设计。
⑦ 政府需要限制学生数据的收集，并排除那些不能确保隐私的公司。
⑧ 教师应当继续作为最终决策者，因为他们比任何平台都更理解语境、情绪和进步。
⑨ 一项平衡的政策会强调人的判断，同时仍允许有用的软件辅助日常练习。
⑩ 如果学校采纳这种方法，AI 将不会取代教师；它会成为众多实用工具中的一种。
⑪ 因此，问题并不是 AI 是否会到来，而是我们准备如何治理它，避免被它反过来治理。

## 语法点示例

**一般将来时 ★★★ · `will` / `be going to` / `shall`**

- 句 ①: `corporate AI tools will dominate many classrooms` — `will` 预测未来趋势。
- 句 ④: `Schools are going to seek tools...` — `be going to` 表较明确的未来打算 / 趋势。
- 句 ⑦: `Governments will need to restrict...` — `will need to` 表未来必须采取的措施。
- 句 ⑧: `Teachers shall remain...` — `shall` 用在正式论述中，带有规范性 / 应然色彩。
- 句 ⑪: `how we are going to govern it` — `be going to` 强调接下来要如何处理已出现的趋势。

# 2026-05-11 · NARRATIVE · Maria, the Consultant

> 体裁: narrative（习惯性叙述）| 字数: ~270 | 新词: 17/17 | 语法点: 一般现在时 #1

## 短文

① Maria works as a consultant in the financial sector.
② Her job requires her to assess complex business situations and interpret data for clients.
③ Each morning, she reviews her contracts and proceeds with a careful approach to every project.
④ She believes in one core principle: never assume anything without evidence.
⑤ This principle guides how she works.
⑥ When companies need help, they often turn to Maria because her judgement is evident in every report she writes.
⑦ Her income depends on the labour she puts in.
⑧ She estimates her workload weekly and distributes tasks among her small team.
⑨ Every project starts with a clear concept, which she derives from talking to the client about their real needs.
⑩ From this concept, she establishes a plan, a budget, and a timeline.
⑪ Finance is at the heart of her work.
⑫ She helps companies that operate across different sectors — from technology to manufacturing.
⑬ The estimate she gives a client is rarely off by more than five percent, and this accuracy is evident to anyone who reads her final reports.
⑭ When a contract is signed, Maria proceeds quickly.
⑮ She does not waste time.
⑯ Her approach is to gather data, interpret it honestly, and present the truth — even when the truth is uncomfortable.
⑰ That is the principle she lives by, and it is why companies come back to her year after year.

## 中文翻译

① 玛丽亚在金融领域担任顾问。
② 她的工作要求她评估复杂的商业情况，并为客户解读数据。
③ 每天早上，她审阅自己的合同，并以谨慎的方式推进每个项目。
④ 她信奉一条核心原则：没有证据时绝不臆断。
⑤ 这条原则指导着她的工作方式。
⑥ 当企业需要帮助时，他们常常向玛丽亚求助，因为她的判断力在她写的每份报告里都显而易见。
⑦ 她的收入取决于她付出的劳动。
⑧ 她每周估算自己的工作量，并在小团队里分配任务。
⑨ 每个项目都从一个清晰的概念开始——这个概念是她通过和客户讨论真实需求得出的。
⑩ 基于这个概念，她制定计划、预算和时间表。
⑪ 财务是她工作的核心。
⑫ 她服务的公司横跨多个行业——从科技到制造业。
⑬ 她给客户的估算很少误差超过 5%，这种精准任何读过她最终报告的人都能看出来。
⑭ 合同一旦签订，玛丽亚就会迅速推进。
⑮ 她从不浪费时间。
⑯ 她的方法是收集数据、诚实地解读，并呈现真相——即使真相令人不安。
⑰ 这就是她秉持的原则，也是企业年复一年回头找她的原因。

## 语法点示例

**一般现在时 ★★★ · 真理 / 习惯 / 状态**

- 句 ①: `Maria works as a consultant` —— 习惯性身份 / 职业（不是单次事件）
- 句 ⑤: `This principle guides how she works.` —— 一般真理（持续作用）
- 句 ⑪: `Finance is at the heart of her work.` —— 状态
- 句 ⑰: `... it is why companies come back to her year after year.` —— 习惯 / 规律

整篇没有一处过去式 —— Simple Present 在 narrative 里专门用来表达**人物的常态**而非**单一事件**。

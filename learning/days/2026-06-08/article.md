# 2026-06-08 · NARRATIVE · The Clinic After the Flood

> 体裁: narrative | 字数: ~230 | 新词: 17/17 | 语法点: 现在完成时 #9

## 短文

① Since the flood, Lina has occupied the back room of the village clinic as a temporary office.
② She has undertaken a quiet investigation into why the clinic's emergency plan failed during its most difficult phase.
③ Her principal hypothesis is simple: the internal warning mechanism worked, but no one knew who had the status to act on it.
④ For three weeks, she has compared the clinic's medical output with the number of families asking for welfare support.
⑤ The figures have exposed a painful gap: elderly patients have received medicine, but many have not yet received clean water.
⑥ Prior reports blamed the storm alone, yet Lina has found a parallel problem in the finance records.
⑦ A small sum had been reserved for transport, but it has already been spent on repairing the generator.
⑧ When the nurse Mei saw the records, she said the overall picture was worse than anyone had assumed.
⑨ Lina has just called a meeting with the doctor, the driver, and the town officer to resolve the confusion.
⑩ During the meeting, each person has explained what they did, what they missed, and what they still need.
⑪ The subsequent plan is modest: one team will deliver water, while another will check patients who have been waiting at home.
⑫ By evening, Lina has not solved every problem, but she has turned a broken system into a clear list of duties.
